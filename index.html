About Sudoku


Sudoku is the one of the puzzle games whose popularity is increasing day by day.This game is being loved by people from any age group. In this game we have a grid in which we have to fill the digits 1 to 9 in such a way that no digit repeats in a row,in a column or in a particular grid section itself. It needs logic to complete the whole grid. Here we are providing you with our version of sudoku puzzle.We hope you will definately enjoy. Happy Playing!!



How to play


  • 1.Click on getPuzzle button to get a sudoku puzzle.
  • 2.Set timer and level foe the game.
  • 3.Use keypad provided to input to particular box in grid.
  • 4.To change number in particular block use undo button.
  • 5.In case you want to start solving puzzle again we have reset all button.
  • 6.Timer will show remaining time.
  • 7.If you are unable to solve we are providing you solution you can see it using solution button.
